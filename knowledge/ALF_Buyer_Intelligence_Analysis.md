# ASSISTED LIVING BUYER INTELLIGENCE ANALYSIS

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This analysis provides actionable intelligence on the competitive landscape for assisted living M&A transactions, focusing on buyer behavior patterns, competitive advantages, and strategic positioning opportunities. The framework identifies specific tactics for competing effectively against REITs, private equity funds, and strategic buyers in target markets.

## I. REIT BUYER COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

### Major Healthcare REIT Platforms

#### **WELLTOWER (NYSE: WELL) - Market Leader Analysis**
**Investment Thesis & Strategy:**
- **Portfolio Focus:** High-quality seniors housing and healthcare real estate
- **Geographic Concentration:** Primary markets, high-barrier locations
- **Capital Deployment:** $2-3B annually in acquisitions and development
- **Preferred Structures:** Triple-net lease arrangements, sale-leaseback transactions

**Competitive Advantages:**
- **Scale & Capital:** $60B+ enterprise value, investment-grade credit rating
- **Cost of Capital:** Unsecured credit facilities at 150-200bps over Treasury
- **Management Platform:** Deep relationships with premier operating partners
- **Market Intelligence:** Sophisticated analytics and market research capabilities

**Competitive Vulnerabilities:**
- **Committee Decision-Making:** Slower approval process for complex deals
- **Size Constraints:** Focus on larger transactions ($25M+), miss smaller opportunities
- **Public Market Pressure:** Quarterly earnings focus may limit strategic flexibility
- **Geographic Concentration:** Portfolio risk in primary markets during downturns

**Competitive Response Strategies:**
- **Speed Advantage:** Target 30-45 day due diligence vs REIT 60-90 day processes
- **Size Focus:** Target $10-25M transactions below REIT threshold preferences
- **Value-Add Positioning:** Emphasize operational improvement vs stabilized acquisitions
- **Relationship Leverage:** Direct seller relationships vs institutional processes

#### **VENTAS (NYSE: VTR) - Platform Analysis**
**Investment Characteristics:**
- **Portfolio Strategy:** Diversified healthcare real estate platform
- **Deal Preferences:** Stabilized assets with growth potential
- **Partnership Approach:** Joint ventures with operating companies
- **Capital Allocation:** Selective acquisition pace, focus on quality

**Competitive Intelligence:**
- **Deal Size Range:** $20M-$100M+ individual transactions
- **Market Positioning:** Core-plus investment strategy
- **Operational Philosophy:** Partner with best-in-class operators
- **Development Activity:** Limited to high-conviction markets

#### **HEALTHCARE REALTY TRUST (NYSE: HR) - Emerging Player**
**Strategic Focus:**
- **Medical Office Emphasis:** Core competency in healthcare facilities
- **Senior Housing Entry:** Growing allocation to seniors housing sector
- **Market Approach:** Secondary market focus for yield enhancement
- **Capital Efficiency:** Disciplined approach to capital deployment

### REIT Decision-Making Framework

#### **INVESTMENT COMMITTEE PROCESS**
**Phase 1: Initial Screening (Weeks 1-2)**
- Asset quality assessment against investment criteria
- Market analysis and demographic validation
- Preliminary financial modeling and returns analysis
- Competitive bid evaluation and positioning

**Phase 2: Due Diligence (Weeks 3-8)**
- Third-party reports: appraisal, environmental, engineering
- Legal and regulatory compliance review
- Market studies and competitive analysis
- Operator evaluation and relationship assessment

**Phase 3: Investment Committee Approval (Weeks 9-12)**
- Final investment recommendation presentation
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Capital allocation priority ranking
- Board of directors approval for larger transactions

**Speed Advantage Opportunities:**
- **Streamlined Process:** 30-day close capability vs 90-day REIT timeline
- **Decision Authority:** Direct decision-making vs committee structure
- **Limited Contingencies:** Reduced third-party report requirements
- **Financing Pre-Approval:** Pre-qualified capital sources
