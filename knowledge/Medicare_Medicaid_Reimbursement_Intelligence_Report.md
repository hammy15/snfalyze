## 1. Medicare Reimbursement Enhancement Framework for SNF Operations

### Current Medicare SNF Payment Structure

#### Patient-Driven Payment Model (PDPM) - Effective October 2019
- **Key Components:**
  - PT (Physical Therapy): 16 case-mix groups
  - OT (Occupational Therapy): 16 case-mix groups  
  - SLP (Speech-Language Pathology): 12 case-mix groups
  - Nursing: 25 case-mix groups
  - Non-Therapy Ancillary (NTA): 6 case-mix groups

#### PDPM Optimization Strategies

**1. Case Mix Optimization**
- **Clinical Documentation Improvement (CDI)**
  - Primary diagnosis optimization for highest RUG category
  - Secondary diagnosis capture for comorbidity adjustments
  - Functional status assessment accuracy (Section GG)
  - Cognitive status documentation (BIMS scores)

**2. Therapy Service Optimization**
- **Variable Per Diem Adjustment (VPA) Maximization**
  - Days 1-20: 100% therapy payment
  - Days 21-100: Graduated reduction based on case mix
  - Strategic discharge timing to maximize VPA periods

**3. Non-Therapy Ancillary (NTA) Enhancement**
- **High-NTA Categories:**
  - Extensive Services (6.92 case mix index)
  - High Plus (2.53 case mix index)
  - High (1.64 case mix index)
- **Documentation Requirements:**
  - HIV/AIDS diagnoses
  - Isolation precautions
  - Dialysis services
  - Chemotherapy/IV medications

### Medicare Advantage (MA) Revenue Strategies

**1. Risk Adjustment Optimization**
- **Hierarchical Condition Categories (HCC) Coding**
  - Annual HCC gap analysis
  - Physician education on documentation
  - Mid-year diagnosis capture initiatives

**2. Star Ratings Performance**
- **Key Metrics for SNFs:**
  - Health Outcomes (25% weight)
