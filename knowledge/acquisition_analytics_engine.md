## 1. Deal Evaluation Methodologies & Financial Analysis {#deal-evaluation}

### Core Valuation Frameworks

#### A. Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Analysis
**Purpose**: Determine intrinsic value based on future cash flows
**Components**:
- Revenue projections (5-10 year forecast)
- Operating margin analysis
- Capital expenditure requirements
- Working capital changes
- Terminal value calculation
- Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC)

**Template Structure**:
```
Year 1-5 Projections:
├── Revenue Growth Assumptions
├── EBITDA Margins
├── Tax Rates
├── Capital Intensity
├── Working Capital % of Sales
└── Terminal Growth Rate
```

#### B. Comparable Company Analysis (Comps)
**Metrics Framework**:
- Enterprise Value/Revenue multiples
- EV/EBITDA multiples
- Price/Earnings ratios
- Price/Book value
- Industry-specific metrics (e.g., EV/Subscribers, Price/Sales)

#### C. Precedent Transaction Analysis
**Key Elements**:
- Transaction premiums by sector
- Deal size correlation analysis
- Strategic vs. financial buyer premiums
- Market timing impact on valuations
- Control premium analysis

### Advanced Valuation Models

#### Monte Carlo Simulation Framework
**Variables to Model**:
- Revenue growth volatility
- Margin compression/expansion
- Market multiple fluctuations
- Integration cost variations
- Synergy realization probability

#### Real Options Valuation
**Application Areas**:
- Growth options embedded in target
- Abandonment options
- Expansion options
- Technology platform options

### Synergy Analysis Framework

#### Revenue Synergies
- Cross-selling opportunities
- Market expansion potential
- Product/service bundling
- Channel optimization
- Brand enhancement

#### Cost Synergies
- Operational consolidation
- Technology integration
- Procurement optimization
- Overhead reduction
- Tax optimization

**Synergy Probability Matrix**:
```
Synergy Type | Probability | Timeline | Value Range
Revenue      | 60-70%      | 12-24mo  | 2-8% of revenue
Cost         | 80-90%      | 6-18mo   | 5-15% of costs
Tax          | 90-95%      | 3-12mo   | 1-3% of revenue
```
