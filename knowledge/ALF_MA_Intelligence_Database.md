## V. SENIOR LIVING TRANSACTION STRUCTURES & FINANCING PATTERNS

### Deal Structure Evolution

#### **OWNERSHIP STRUCTURE TRENDS**

**Fee Simple Ownership:**
- *Market Share:* ~60% of transactions
- *Buyer Profile:* Strategic operators, some PE funds
- *Advantages:* Full control, development rights, appreciation upside
- *Considerations:* Higher capital requirements, operational responsibility

**Triple Net Lease (NNN):**
- *Market Share:* ~25% of transactions (growing)
- *Buyer Profile:* REITs seeking stable cash flows
- *Advantages:* Predictable returns, tenant operational responsibility
- *Structure:* 10-20 year initial terms, escalation clauses, corporate guarantees

**Ground Lease Arrangements:**
- *Market Share:* ~10% of transactions
- *Buyer Profile:* Land-constrained markets, development projects
- *Advantages:* Lower capital requirements for operators
- *Structure:* 50-99 year terms, participation in revenue upside

**Joint Venture Structures:**
- *Market Share:* ~5% of transactions
- *Buyer Profile:* Development projects, risk-sharing arrangements
- *Advantages:* Shared capital requirements and risk
- *Structure:* Institutional partner + operating partner

#### **EMERGING STRUCTURE INNOVATIONS**

**Sale-Leaseback Transactions:**
- *Growth Trend:* 15-20% annual increase in transaction volume
- *Driver Factors:* Capital efficiency, tax optimization, growth capital
- *Typical Terms:* 10-15 year initial lease, 1-2% annual escalations
- *Market Leaders:* Healthcare REITs as purchasers

**Master Lease Portfolios:**
- *Transaction Size:* $100M-$1B+ portfolio deals
- *Structure Benefits:* Portfolio diversification, operational efficiency
- *Credit Enhancement:* Corporate guarantees, cross-default provisions
- *Growth Catalyst:* Platform companies seeking growth capital

### Financing Patterns & Sources

#### **DEBT FINANCING LANDSCAPE**

**Agency Debt Programs:**
- **HUD 232:** Federal insurance for healthcare facilities
  - *Terms:* Up to 35 year amortization, 75-85% LTV
  - *Rates:* Treasury + 200-300bps depending on deal characteristics
  - *Process:* 6-12 month approval timeline

- **Fannie Mae/Freddie Mac:** Multifamily programs for qualifying assets
  - *Terms:* 10-30 year amortization, 75-80% LTV  
  - *Rates:* Treasury + 150-250bps
  - *Process:* 3-6 month approval timeline

**Commercial Bank Financing:**
- *Market Share:* ~40% of senior housing debt
- *Terms:* 5-10 year terms, 70-75% LTV typical
- *Rates:* SOFR + 200-400bps depending on risk profile
- *Advantages:* Speed of execution, relationship-based

**Life Insurance Company Debt:**
- *Market Share:* ~25% of senior housing debt
- *Terms:* 10-20 year fixed rate, 65-75% LTV
- *Rates:* Treasury + 150-300bps for stabilized assets
- *Advantages:* Long-term fixed rate, stable capital source

#### **EQUITY FINANCING TRENDS**

**REIT Capital Sources:**
- **Unsecured Corporate Credit:** Primary acquisition financing tool
- **Equity Offerings:** ATM programs for growth capital
- **Joint Ventures:** Risk-sharing with institutional partners
- **Development Financing:** Construction-to-perm arrangements

**Private Equity Capital:**
- **Fund Commitments:** Institutional investor capital calls
- **High-Leverage Structures:** 60-70% LTV typical for value-add
- **Mezzanine Financing:** Higher-cost capital for complex deals
- **Bridge Financing:** Short-term acquisition/improvement capital
