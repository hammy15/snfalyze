## Medicare Advantage Contract Benchmarking

### National Payment Rate Benchmarks (2024-2025)

#### Per-Member Per-Month (PMPM) Rates by Service Category

**Skilled Nursing Facility Services:**
- **National Average:** $85-$125 PMPM
- **Regional Variations:**
  - Northeast: $110-$150 PMPM
  - Southeast: $70-$100 PMPM  
  - Midwest: $75-$110 PMPM
  - West Coast: $120-$165 PMPM

**Episode-Based Payment Rates:**
- **Short-Stay Episodes (1-20 days):** $8,500-$15,000
- **Medium-Stay Episodes (21-50 days):** $18,000-$28,000
- **Long-Stay Episodes (51+ days):** $35,000-$55,000

#### Risk Adjustment Factors

**Hierarchical Condition Category (HCC) Multipliers:**
- **Base Population:** 1.0x multiplier
- **High-Risk Population:** 1.5-2.2x multiplier
- **Specialized Care Needs:** 2.5-3.5x multiplier
- **End-of-Life Care:** 4.0-6.0x multiplier

### Quality-Based Payment Adjustments

#### Star Rating Performance Bonuses

**5-Star Facilities:**
- **Bonus Range:** 15-25% above base rates
- **Quality Achievement:** $2,000-$4,000 per member annually
- **Network Preferred Status:** Priority placement and referrals

**4-Star Facilities:**
- **Bonus Range:** 8-15% above base rates
- **Performance Incentives:** $1,000-$2,500 per member annually
- **Standard Network Position:** Regular referral patterns

**3-Star and Below:**
- **Rate Impact:** Base rates or 5-10% reduction
- **Improvement Requirements:** Performance improvement plans
- **Network Status:** Monitoring and potential termination

#### Clinical Quality Metrics

**Primary Performance Indicators:**
- **30-Day Readmission Rate:** Target <15% (10% payment bonus)
- **Discharge to Community:** Target >65% (8% payment bonus)
- **Length of Stay Efficiency:** Target <25 days average (5% bonus)
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Target >85% (7% payment bonus)

**Secondary Quality Measures:**
- **Infection Control:** <5% HAI rate (3% bonus)
- **Medication Management:** <10% adverse events (2% bonus)
- **Staff Retention:** <35% annual turnover (4% bonus)
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Zero deficiencies (6% bonus)

## Value-Based Care Contract Models

### Shared Savings Programs

#### Medicare Advantage Shared Savings

**Program Structure:**
- **Baseline Establishment:** Historical cost benchmarking
- **Savings Threshold:** 2-5% minimum savings requirement
- **Sharing Percentage:** 25-50% of savings to provider
- **Quality Gates:** 85%+ quality metric achievement

**Financial Framework:**
```
Shared Savings = (Baseline Costs - Actual Costs - Minimum Savings Threshold) × Sharing Percentage × Quality Multiplier
```

**Example Calculation:**
- Baseline Costs: $2,000,000
- Actual Costs: $1,750,000  
- Savings Achieved: $250,000 (12.5%)
- Minimum Threshold: $100,000 (5%)
- Provider Share: ($250,000 - $100,000) × 40% = $60,000

#### Risk-Based Arrangements

**1. Upside-Only Models**
- **Savings Potential:** 10-25% above baseline
- **Quality Requirements:** 90%+ performance metrics
- **Financial Risk:** Limited to opportunity cost
- **Implementation:** Entry-level value-based care

**2. Two-Sided Risk Models**
- **Upside Potential:** 20-40% profit opportunity
- **Downside Risk:** 10-20% loss exposure
- **Risk Corridors:** Graduated risk/reward tiers
- **Stop-Loss:** Maximum annual exposure limits
