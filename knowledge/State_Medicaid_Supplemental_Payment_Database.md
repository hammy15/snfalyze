# State Medicaid Supplemental Payment Programs - Intelligence Database

## Executive Overview

This database provides comprehensive state-by-state intelligence on Medicaid supplemental payment opportunities, provider fee programs, and enhancement strategies for skilled nursing facility operations. Data includes payment amounts, eligibility requirements, quality metrics, and strategic optimization approaches.

---

## Tier 1 States - Highest Opportunity (>$1,500 per bed annually)

### Texas - Long-Term Care Quality Incentive Program (LTCQIP)

**Program Structure:**
- **Total Pool:** $50 million annually
- **Payment Method:** Quality-based performance payments
- **Eligibility:** Licensed nursing facilities serving Medicaid residents
- **Minimum Requirements:** 75% occupancy rate, quality metrics compliance

**Payment Tiers:**
- **Tier 1 (Top 25%):** $2,100 per Medicaid bed annually
- **Tier 2 (26-50%):** $1,650 per Medicaid bed annually  
- **Tier 3 (51-75%):** $1,200 per Medicaid bed annually
- **Tier 4 (Bottom 25%):** $750 per Medicaid bed annually

**Quality Metrics (100 points total):**
- MDS Quality Measures (40 points)
- Five-Star Rating (25 points)
- Infection Control (20 points)
- Staff Turnover (15 points)

**Optimization Strategies:**
- Focus on MDS accuracy and completeness
- Implement infection prevention protocols
- Develop staff retention programs
- Monitor and improve Five-Star ratings

### California - Quality Assurance Fee (QAF) Program

**Program Structure:**
- **Provider Assessment:** $7,200 per certified bed annually
- **Federal Match:** 2:1 ratio ($14,400 return)
- **Net Benefit:** $7,200 per bed profit
- **Program Duration:** Multi-year authorizations

**Payment Distribution:**
- **Base Payment:** 70% distributed equally per bed
- **Quality Incentive:** 20% based on performance metrics
- **Access Payment:** 10% for rural/underserved facilities

**Quality Metrics:**
- Nursing Hours per Resident Day
- Staff Turnover Rates
- Customer Satisfaction Scores
- Regulatory Compliance History

**Strategic Considerations:**
- Cash flow management during assessment period
- Quality improvement investment planning
- Long-term sustainability planning

### New York - Medicaid Redesign Team (MRT) Value-Based Payments

**Program Components:**
- **Managed Long-Term Care (MLTC) Arrangements**
- **Value-Based Payment (VBP) Contracts** 
- **Capital Investment Supplements**
- **Quality Incentive Pools**

**VBP Payment Structure:**
- **Level 1:** Fee-for-service with bonus (5-8% upside)
- **Level 2:** Shared savings (10-15% upside)
- **Level 3:** Population-based payments (20-25% upside)
- **Level 4:** Integrated finance and delivery (30%+ upside)

**Performance Metrics:**
- 30-day readmission rates
- Discharge to community percentages
- Total cost of care management
- Member satisfaction scores

**Enhancement Opportunities:**
- MLTC partnership development
- Care coordination improvements
- Population health management
- Technology infrastructure investment

## Tier 2 States - Moderate Opportunity ($750-$1,500 per bed annually)

### Florida - Low-Income Pool (LIP) Program

**Program Overview:**
- **Total Fund:** $2.1 billion federal matching
- **SNF Allocation:** ~$150 million annually
- **Payment Basis:** Medicaid utilization and quality metrics
- **Distribution:** Quarterly payments

**Payment Categories:**
- **Medicaid Shortfall:** Covers underpayments vs costs
- **Uncompensated Care:** Charity care and bad debt
- **Quality Improvement:** Performance-based bonuses
- **Access Enhancement:** Rural/underserved supplements

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Minimum Medicaid census: 30%
- Quality reporting compliance
- Financial auditing participation
- Network adequacy commitments

### Illinois - Assessment-Based Supplemental Programs

**Nursing Facility Assessment:**
- **Assessment Rate:** $5,200 per bed annually
- **Federal Match:** 1.5:1 average ratio
- **Net Distribution:** $7,800 per bed return
- **Quality Holdback:** 10% for performance incentives

**Quality Incentive Measures:**
- Pressure ulcer prevention rates
- Antipsychotic medication reduction
- Staff-to-resident ratios
- Survey deficiency scores

### Ohio - Medicaid Managed Care Value Incentives

**Managed Care Integration:**
- **MCO Partnership Bonuses:** Up to $1,200 per bed
- **Quality Achievement:** Performance-based payments
- **Care Coordination:** Episode-based supplements
- **Technology Adoption:** Infrastructure support payments

**Payment Methodology:**
- Base rate negotiations with MCOs
- Quality bonus calculations
- Risk-sharing arrangements
- Outcome-based adjustments

## Financial Modeling Framework

### Revenue Enhancement Calculations

**Base Formula:**
Enhanced Revenue = (Supplemental Payment × Eligible Beds) + (Quality Bonus × Performance Score) + (Access Supplement × Rural/Underserved Status)

**Example Calculation (Texas LTCQIP):**
- 100-bed facility, 80 Medicaid beds
- Tier 2 performance rating
- Enhanced Revenue = ($1,650 × 80) + Quality Bonus = $132,000 annually

### ROI Analysis Template

**Investment Requirements:**
