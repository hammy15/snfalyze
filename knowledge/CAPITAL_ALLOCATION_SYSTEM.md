## CAPITAL ALLOCATION PRIORITY FRAMEWORK

### 1️⃣ CAPITAL ALLOCATION CATEGORIES
All capital deployment must fall into one category:

**A. Stabilization Capital**
- Purpose: Protect existing investments
- Priority: Highest
- ROI: Downside protection

**B. High-ROI Operational Lift**
- Purpose: Operational improvements with measurable returns
- Priority: High
- ROI: Quantified efficiency gains

**C. Strategic Expansion**
- Purpose: Portfolio growth and market position
- Priority: Medium
- ROI: Long-term value creation

**D. Defensive Risk Reduction**
- Purpose: Reduce portfolio vulnerabilities
- Priority: Medium-High
- ROI: Risk mitigation value

**E. Maintenance / CapEx**
- Purpose: Asset preservation and compliance
- Priority: Required
- ROI: Asset value protection

**F. Opportunistic / Speculative**
- Purpose: High-risk, high-reward opportunities
- Priority: Lowest
- ROI: Uncertain but potentially high
