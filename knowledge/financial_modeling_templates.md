## Comparable Company Analysis Template

### Peer Universe Selection Criteria
**Primary Criteria**:
- Industry: Same/adjacent industries
- Business Model: Similar revenue streams
- Size: 0.5x - 2.0x target revenue
- Geography: Similar market exposure
- Growth Stage: Comparable maturity

**Secondary Criteria**:
- Profitability profile
- Capital intensity
- End market exposure
- Competitive positioning
