## 2. MEMORY CARE PREMIUM VALUATIONS & MARKET DIFFERENTIALS

### Research Framework
- Premium Analysis: MC vs AL base pricing differentials
- Market Drivers: Demographics, regulatory environment, competition
- Regional Variations: State-level licensing impacts

**PRELIMINARY FINDINGS:**

**Memory Care Premium Structure:**
- Industry standard: 15-35% premium over standard assisted living
- High-barrier markets: 40-50% premiums in markets with restrictive licensing
- Geographic variation driven by:
  - State regulatory complexity
  - Local market competition density
  - Demographic concentration (age 85+ population)

**Market Driver Analysis:**
- **Regulatory Barriers:** States with specialized MC licensing see higher premiums
- **Land Constraints:** Urban markets with limited development capacity
- **Operator Consolidation:** Markets dominated by 3-5 operators show pricing power

## 4. REIT VS PRIVATE EQUITY BUYER DYNAMICS & PREFERENCES

### Buyer Profile Analysis

**REIT CHARACTERISTICS:**
- **Scale Requirements:** Typically $25M+ transaction minimums
- **Market Preference:** Core/Core-plus stabilized assets
- **Geographic Focus:** Primary markets, portfolio scale opportunities
- **Hold Period:** Long-term hold (10+ years)

**PRIVATE EQUITY CHARACTERISTICS:**  
- **Deal Size Range:** $10M-$100M+ range
- **Value-Add Focus:** Operational improvements, expansion opportunities
- **Hold Period:** 3-7 year target exits
- **Return Targets:** 15-25% IRR expectations

**STRATEGIC BUYERS (Operating Companies):**
- **Portfolio Integration:** Synergy-driven acquisitions  
- **Market Expansion:** Geographic density strategies
- **Operational Leverage:** Management platform scalability

### Competitive Dynamics
- **REIT Advantages:** Low-cost capital, patient capital structure
- **PE Advantages:** Speed of execution, operational expertise
- **Strategic Advantages:** Management synergies, market knowledge

## 5. SENIOR LIVING TRANSACTION STRUCTURES & FINANCING PATTERNS

### Deal Structure Evolution

**OWNERSHIP STRUCTURES:**
- **Fee Simple:** Traditional ownership model
- **Triple Net Lease:** Growing REIT preference for tax efficiency
- **Ground Lease:** Land-constrained market solutions
- **Joint Venture:** Risk-sharing for development projects

**FINANCING TRENDS:**
- **REIT Capital:** Unsecured corporate credit, equity offerings
- **PE Financing:** High-leverage structures (60-70% LTV)
- **Agency Debt:** HUD, Fannie Mae programs for stabilized assets
- **Bridge Financing:** Short-term construction/lease-up capital

### Transaction Timeline Patterns
- **Due Diligence Period:** 45-90 days for institutional buyers
- **Financing Contingency:** 30-45 days for debt arrangements  
- **Regulatory Approval:** 30-180 days (state-dependent)
