# Integration Planning & Execution Playbooks

## Integration Management Office (IMO) Framework

### IMO Charter and Governance Structure

#### Mission Statement
The Integration Management Office (IMO) serves as the central coordination hub for all post-acquisition integration activities, ensuring systematic value creation, risk mitigation, and successful business combination within established timelines and budgets.

#### Core Responsibilities
- [ ] Integration strategy development and execution oversight
- [ ] Cross-functional workstream coordination and management
- [ ] Synergy identification, tracking, and realization assurance
- [ ] Risk assessment, mitigation, and issue escalation
- [ ] Stakeholder communication and change management
- [ ] Performance measurement and reporting
- [ ] Best practice capture and knowledge transfer

#### Governance Structure
```
Integration Steering Committee (ISC)
├── CEO/Business Unit Head (Chair)
├── CFO (Financial Oversight)
├── CHRO (People Integration)
├── CTO (Technology Integration)
├── Chief Strategy Officer (Strategic Alignment)
├── General Counsel (Legal/Compliance)
└── IMO Director (Operational Leadership)

Functional Workstreams:
├── Finance & Accounting
├── Human Resources & Culture
├── Sales & Marketing
├── Operations & Supply Chain
├── Technology & Systems
├── Legal & Compliance
├── Communications & Change Management
└── Synergy Realization
```

### Day 1 Readiness Framework

**Legal and Regulatory**:
- [ ] All closing conditions satisfied and documented
- [ ] Required regulatory approvals obtained and filed

- [ ] Corporate structure and entity registrations updated

- [ ] Banking and financial account access established

**Operational Continuity**:

- [ ] Supply chain and vendor relationships secured

#### Risk and Issue Management
**Risk Categories and Mitigation**:
- [ ] High Impact/High Probability: Immediate action required
- [ ] High Impact/Low Probability: Contingency planning
- [ ] Low Impact/High Probability: Standard mitigation
- [ ] Low Impact/Low Probability: Monitor only

**Escalation Matrix**:
- [ ] Level 1: Workstream lead resolution (0-48 hours)
- [ ] Level 2: IMO director involvement (48-96 hours)
- [ ] Level 3: Steering committee escalation (>96 hours)
- [ ] Level 4: Executive leadership intervention (critical issues)
