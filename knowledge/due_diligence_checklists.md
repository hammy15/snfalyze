# Comprehensive Due Diligence Checklists

## Strategic Due Diligence Checklist

### Market & Industry Analysis
#### Market Dynamics
- [ ] Industry size and growth trends (5-year historical, 5-year forecast)
- [ ] Market segmentation and sub-market analysis
- [ ] Geographic market distribution and regional variations
- [ ] Seasonality and cyclicality patterns
- [ ] Market maturity and lifecycle stage assessment
- [ ] Key market drivers and growth catalysts
- [ ] Market disruption risks and emerging technologies

#### Competitive Landscape
- [ ] Market share analysis (company and top 5-10 competitors)
- [ ] Competitive positioning matrix (price, quality, service, innovation)
- [ ] Competitor financial performance benchmarking
- [ ] Competitive advantage sustainability assessment
- [ ] Barriers to entry and exit analysis
- [ ] New entrant threat assessment
- [ ] Substitute product/service risk evaluation

#### Regulatory Environment
- [ ] Current regulatory framework and compliance requirements
- [ ] Pending regulatory changes and impact assessment
- [ ] Licensing and permit requirements
- [ ] Environmental regulations and compliance status
- [ ] Data privacy and security regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- [ ] Industry-specific regulations and standards
- [ ] Political and policy risk assessment

### Strategic Positioning Assessment
#### Value Proposition Analysis
- [ ] Core value proposition definition and differentiation
- [ ] Customer value creation mechanisms
- [ ] Pricing power and elasticity analysis
- [ ] Brand strength and recognition assessment
- [ ] Service/quality superiority evidence
- [ ] Innovation capabilities and R&D effectiveness
- [ ] Distribution channel advantages

#### Business Model Evaluation
- [ ] Revenue model analysis and diversification
- [ ] Cost structure optimization and efficiency
- [ ] Scalability and operating leverage potential
- [ ] Network effects and platform dynamics
- [ ] Asset utilization and capital efficiency
- [ ] Business model resilience and adaptability
- [ ] Digital transformation readiness

## Financial Due Diligence Checklist

### Financial Statement Analysis
#### Income Statement Review
- [ ] Revenue recognition policies and practices
- [ ] Revenue quality assessment (recurring vs. one-time)
- [ ] Gross margin analysis and trends
- [ ] Operating expense categorization and trends
- [ ] EBITDA normalization and quality analysis
- [ ] Non-recurring and extraordinary items identification
- [ ] Tax rate analysis and optimization opportunities

#### Balance Sheet Analysis
- [ ] Asset composition and quality assessment
- [ ] Working capital components and trends
- [ ] Debt structure and maturity profile
- [ ] Off-balance sheet obligations and commitments
- [ ] Related party transactions and balances
- [ ] Contingent liabilities assessment
- [ ] Equity structure and ownership analysis

#### Cash Flow Analysis
- [ ] Operating cash flow generation and quality
- [ ] Free cash flow calculation and trends
- [ ] Cash conversion cycle analysis
- [ ] Capital expenditure requirements and timing
- [ ] Working capital management efficiency
- [ ] Cash flow seasonality and volatility
- [ ] Dividend/distribution sustainability

### Quality of Earnings Analysis
#### Revenue Analysis
- [ ] Revenue recognition timing and methodology
- [ ] Channel stuffing or revenue acceleration indicators
- [ ] Bill-and-hold or consignment arrangements
- [ ] Related party revenue transactions
- [ ] Contract terms and revenue recognition risks
- [ ] Warranty and return provisions adequacy
- [ ] Deferred revenue analysis and trends

#### Cost and Expense Analysis
- [ ] Cost classification accuracy (COGS vs. OpEx)
- [ ] Expense timing and accrual analysis
- [ ] Related party expense transactions
- [ ] Management fee and allocation methodologies
- [ ] Stock-based compensation treatment
- [ ] Restructuring and one-time costs
- [ ] Pension and post-employment benefit obligations

### Debt and Capital Structure
#### Debt Analysis
- [ ] Debt facility terms and covenants
- [ ] Interest rate exposure and hedging strategies
- [ ] Debt maturity and refinancing requirements
- [ ] Guarantee and security provisions
- [ ] Change of control provisions and triggers
- [ ] Financial covenant compliance and headroom
- [ ] Debt capacity and additional borrowing ability

#### Working Capital Deep Dive
- [ ] Accounts receivable aging and collection patterns
- [ ] Inventory composition, turnover, and obsolescence
- [ ] Accounts payable terms and payment patterns
- [ ] Seasonal working capital requirements
- [ ] Working capital normalization adjustments
- [ ] Days sales outstanding and collection efficiency
- [ ] Supplier payment terms and optimization opportunities

- [ ] Process efficiency and automation levels
- [ ] Quality management systems and certifications
- [ ] Continuous improvement programs and culture
- [ ] Operational metrics and KPI tracking

#### Supply Chain Evaluation

- [ ] Supply chain resilience and risk management

### Human Resources Deep Dive
