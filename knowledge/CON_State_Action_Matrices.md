# CON State Action Matrices - Acquisition Decision Framework
## Executive Summary

This document provides state-by-state decision matrices for immediate action planning in Certificate of Need regulatory environments. Each matrix includes risk assessment, opportunity scoring, and specific action recommendations for SNF/ALF acquisition and development.

**Date**: February 11, 2026  
**Purpose**: Tactical Decision Support

## State Classification and Scoring Framework

### Scoring Methodology (1-10 Scale)

**Market Attractiveness (1-10)**:
- Demographics and growth (25%)
- Economic environment (20%)
- Healthcare demand trends (25%)
- Competitive landscape (20%)
- Reimbursement environment (10%)

**Regulatory Environment (1-10)**:
- CON stability/predictability (30%)
- Application complexity (25%)
- Processing timelines (20%)
- Success rates (15%)
- Political risk (10%)

**Investment Priority Score = (Market Attractiveness × 0.6) + (Regulatory Environment × 0.4)**

## Decision Framework Summary

### Go/No-Go Criteria

**Immediate Execution (GO)**:
- Investment Score >8.0
- Low political risk
- Clear regulatory path
- Market growth >3% annually

**Conditional Execution (PROCEED WITH CAUTION)**:
- Investment Score 7.0-8.0  
- Moderate political risk
- Reform uncertainty
- Strong fundamentals

**Monitor Only (NO-GO)**:
- Investment Score <7.0
- High political risk  
- Active reform environment
- Market saturation

**Budget Allocation by Tier**:
- Tier 1 States: 60% of development budget
- Tier 2 States: 25% of development budget  
- Tier 3 States: 10% of development budget
- Tier 4 States: 5% for monitoring only

**Timeline Targets**:
- Tier 1: Execute within 6 months
- Tier 2: Execute within 12 months
- Tier 3: Evaluate annually
- Tier 4: Monitor quarterly
